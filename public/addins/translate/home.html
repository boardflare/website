=BOARDFLARE.TRANSLATE (text, source_lang, target_lang, [model])

text: cell to translate, must be less than 1000 characters. source_lang: language of text, e.g. "fr" for French. target_lang: target language, e.g. "en" for English. model: optional, see models for details.

E.g., to translate French in cell A1 to English: =BOARDFLARE.TRANSLATE(A1, "fr", "en")
